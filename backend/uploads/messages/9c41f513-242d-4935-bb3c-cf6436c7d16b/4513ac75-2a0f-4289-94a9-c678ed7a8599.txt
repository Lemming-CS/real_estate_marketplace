  Full payment-to-promotion flow:

  1. Admin creates an active promotion package.
  2. Seller calls POST /api/v1/payments/promotions/initiate with listing, package, duration,
     and target scope.
  3. Backend validates the listing is owned, active, and published, then creates a promotions
     row in pending_payment and a linked payment_records row in pending.
  4. Seller or admin simulates the mock provider result through POST /api/v1/payments/

  6. On expiry, promotion becomes expired through backend sync logic and the seller gets a
     promotion.expired notification.

  backend/.env.example. No secret or external provider account is required for this MVP flow.
